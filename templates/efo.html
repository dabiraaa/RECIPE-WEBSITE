EFO RIRO(Vegetable stew)

  1. 2 large red bell peppers
  2. 5 scotch bonnet
  3. 2 medium onions
  4. 1/4 cup Palm oil
  5. Locust bean
  6. 1 tasty cube
  7. Salt to taste
  8. 1 bunch of spinach
  9. Dry fish
  10. Ponmo
  11. 1 lb Beef

DIRECTIONS

Blend the peppers and onions not to a puree.Heat a pot and add palm oil. After 3 minutes,add onions then the pepper mix.Add locust bean, seasoning, dry fish,ponmo and beef. Leave for about 5-10 minutes.Rinse and chop spinach and add to it. Stir and it should be ready in 5 minutes.
