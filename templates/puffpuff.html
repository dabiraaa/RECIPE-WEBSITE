PUFFPUFF

INGREDIENTS

  1. 1 cup of flour
  2. 2 tablespoons of sugar
  3. A pinch of yeast
  4. 1 tsp Cinnamon
  5. Salt
  6. Warm water
  7. Vegetable oil for frying

DIRECTIONS

Add the flour to a bowl and create a hole to add the yeast.Mix together. Add the sugar, cinnamon and salt.Then add warm water.Mix to get a good consistency(a thick and sticky batter). Cover with cling film and leave to rise in a warm place. Upon rising, heat oil in a deep pan. Scoop the batter to your desired size and fry in batches. Puffpuff can be served with a blend of cinnamon+sugar.
