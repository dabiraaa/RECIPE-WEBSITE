EGUSI SOUP(MELON SOUP)

INGREDIENTS

  1. Blended melon seeds
  2. 2 red bell peppers
  3. 5 scotch bonnet
  4. 2 medium onions
  5. 1/4 cup Palm oil
  6. Locust bean
  7. 1 tasty cube
  8. Salt to taste
  9. 1 bunch of spinach
  10. Dry fish
  11. Ponmo
  12. 1 lb Beef

DIRECTIONS

Blend the peppers and onions not to a puree.Make a paste of the blended melon seeds with a bit of the pepper mix. Heat water in a pot and add the blended pepper.Add the paste and let it cook for 15 mins. Add palm oil,locust bean, seasoning, dry fish,ponmo and beef. Leave for about 5-10 minutes.Rinse and chop spinach and add to it. Stir and it should be ready in 5 minutes. Egusi soup can be taken with any swallow
